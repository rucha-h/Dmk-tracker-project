💡F2P Strategy Guide
🎯 Core Principles
⬆️
Level up characters FIRST. Maxed characters unlock new activities, quests, and buildings. When a character is ready to level up, stop farming their tokens — you can't stockpile them at cap.
✨
Don't spend Magic on decorations. Save every drop of Magic for: welcoming characters, building attractions, and expanding the Kingdom. Decorations are last priority.
💎
Save Gems for characters, not speed-ups. Premium characters unlock extra quests and rare token sources. Speed-ups waste gems — plan ahead with overnight tasks instead.
🔄
Keep all characters active always. Never leave a character idle. Even if their tokens are maxed, they still earn Magic and XP from tasks.
✨ Magic Farming
🎪
Run parades as often as possible. Each parade earns substantial Magic, Gems, and Tokens. The cooldown is 8 hours — run it twice a day minimum.
😊
Keep the Happiness Meter at Ecstatic. At full happiness, attractions have a higher chance of dropping Rare/Uncommon tokens. Grant visitor wishes constantly.
🏗️
Build all concession stands. Even small stands generate passive Magic. The more you have, the more you earn while away.
🪙 Token Strategy
🎯
Focus on one character at a time. Pick the character blocking your next quest, then farm tokens specifically for them. Spreading across many characters is inefficient.
🏰
Enchant Attractions. Enchanted attractions drop tokens for characters you haven't unlocked yet. Blueprint and Relic Tokens from chests enable this.
📦
Collect from attractions frequently. Don't let attraction timers overflow — you miss earnings. Set a timer to check in every few hours.
🎉 Event Strategy
⏰
Prioritize event currency tasks above all else. During events, identify which characters earn bonus event currency and keep them on those tasks continuously.
🏠
Send non-event characters home. Use the "Send Home" feature to free up activity slots for event characters. Non-event characters earn Magic passively at the castle instead.
🗓️
Calculate daily currency needed. Divide total event currency goal by remaining days. If you're falling behind, increase task frequency. Tower Challenge rewards Maleficent Coins to exchange for character tokens.
💎 Gem Strategy (F2P) & ✨ Dreamsparks (Act 6+)
📅
Collect daily rewards every single day. Daily login rewards include Gems and sometimes Enchanted Chests. Streak bonuses improve over time — don't break your streak.
⬆️
Level up characters for Gem rewards. Every character level-up grants Gems. Completing a character's full collection also awards Gems — this is the main F2P Gem source.
📢
Watch daily announcements. Free Gems from watching in-game announcements. Small amount but adds up weekly. Check the notification bell daily.
🏆 Token Rarities
🎖️
Tokens drop in 5 rarities: Common   Uncommon   Rare   Epic   Legendary

Higher-level character upgrades require rarer tokens. Ecstatic happiness boosts rare token drop rates from attractions.
